What I Do Best

I specialize in the Zoho One ecosystem, transforming messy, manual processes into streamlined, automated workflows. Whether it's building custom applications in Zoho Creator, architecting CRM pipelines, or generating high-level insights via Zoho Analytics, I focus on three core pillars:
